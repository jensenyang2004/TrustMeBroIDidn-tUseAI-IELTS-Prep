# Trust Me Bro I Didn't Use AI - IELTS Prep 🎓

An open-source, AI-powered IELTS writing preparation tool designed to help you achieve your target band score. Powered by Google's Gemini AI, this application provides granular, highly specific feedback on full essays and generates targeted micro-exercises to build fundamental writing skills.

## ✨ Features

### 1. Full Essay Grader (Task 1 & Task 2)

Submit your full IELTS writing tasks and receive comprehensive, examiner-style feedback:

* **Band Scoring:** Get an estimated overall band score.
* **Four Pillars Breakdown:** Detailed comments on Task Achievement/Response, Coherence & Cohesion, Lexical Resource, and Grammatical Range & Accuracy.
* **Line-by-Line Corrections:** Direct fixes for grammatical and vocabulary errors in your original text.
* **Refined Version:** A fully rewritten, high-scoring version of your essay to model native-level flow and advanced vocabulary.
* **Actionable Improvement Notes:** Specific, constructive advice on what exactly you need to enhance to reach the next band.



### 2. AI-Generated Micro-Exercises

Short on time? Focus on specific weaknesses with AI-generated short essay practice.

* **Targeted Skills:** Generate prompts focused on specific areas like *Lexical Resource*, *Grammatical Range*, *Argument Development*, or *Cohesion & Coherence*.
* **Instant Rating:** Submit your short responses and let Gemini evaluate and rate your localized performance immediately.

### 3. Personalized Learning Vault

Don't lose track of the great feedback you get.

* **Store Improvement Notes:** Save specific corrections, high-level vocabulary, and advanced sentence structures directly to your profile.
* **Review & Remember:** Build a custom repository of phrases and structures to memorize and deploy on test day.
